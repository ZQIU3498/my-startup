Welcome to Foodie, a creative vegetarian restaurant dedicated to organic cuisine. Nestled in the heart of Paris, Foodie offers a unique dining experience where every dish is crafted with the freshest, locally-sourced organic ingredients.

Our menu is a delightful fusion of global flavors and innovative culinary techniques, ensuring that each meal is not only healthy but also bursting with taste.

At Foodie, we believe in promoting sustainability and well-being through our food, making it the perfect spot for health-conscious diners and food enthusiasts alike. Join us for a meal that nourishes both the body and the soul!
